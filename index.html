  • Exercise 5.2.4

    Q1. Flights had an arrival delay of two or more hours?
    Ans1: filter(flights, arr_delay >= 120)

    Q2. Flights flew to Houston (IAH or HOU)?
    Ans2: ilter(flights, dest %in% c("IAH","HOU"))

    Q3. Flights operated by United, American, or Delta?
    Ans3: filter(flights, carrier %in% c("UA","AA","DL"))

    Q4. Flights departed in summer (July, August, and September)?
    filter(flights, (month == 7 | month == 8 | month == 9))

    Q5. Flights arrived more than two hours late, but didn't leave late
    Ans5: filter(flights, arr_delay > 120 & dep_delay <= 0 )

    Q6. Flights were delayed by at least an hour, but made up over 30 minutes in flight?
    Ans6: filter(flights, dep_delay >= 60 & arr_delay <= 30)

    Q7. Flights departed between midnight and 6am (inclusive)?
    filter(flights, dep_time %in% c(seq(1,600)))
